Short Movie - Narciso

Short Movie

Narciso

Berlin
August 2024
PERSONAL PROJECT

Runtime: 3’15” Shot on Sony Alpha 7s III

This short film was conceived and produced entirely in Berlin. The idea was born from a conversation between students of literature, design, and law about Greek myths and the symbolic resonance they still hold today. Narcissus is a modern reinterpretation of the myth, aiming to preserve and convey the same visual magic of the ancient tales and the universality of their meaning. The entire project was developed over the course of about three months, with only two days dedicated to on-site filming.

“Our identity is shaped in a delicate balance between who we truly are and the image we project in the eyes of others. Loving only himself, Narcissus becomes lost in his own reflection, fooling himself into believing that this love can fill his emptiness and bring fulfillment.„
