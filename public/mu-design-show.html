MU Design Show

Design System

During my Erasmus exchange, I was selected as the Art Director to define the visual identity for the "Media University Design Show." I had the opportunity to coordinate a team of students in designing all visual assets and creating the event's design system.
The MU Design Show is an annual event held at the end of the academic year at Media University in Berlin. This exhibition offers students a platform to showcase their creative works to the public. The exhibition includes a variety of projects, highlighting the innovative and artistic talent of the university's students.

Credits
