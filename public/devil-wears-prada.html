The Devil Wears Prada

AI Generation

This project explores the parallels between branding and religious devotion. Using AI-generated fashion imagery, it visualizes hypothetical collaborations between global brands and religious symbols, blending commercial and spiritual iconography. The goal is to provoke reflection on how brands shape identity, beliefs, and consumer behavior—much like faith does with its followers.
