Narciso

Short Movie

This short film was conceived and produced entirely in Berlin. The idea originated from a conversation about Greek myths and the importance of their symbolic resonance. "Narciso" is a modern reinterpretation aimed at preserving the visual magic of ancient tales and the universality of their meaning. The entire project was developed in three months, with only two days dedicated to on-location shooting.
