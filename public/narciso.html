Narciso

Short Movie

This short film was conceived and produced entirely in Berlin. The idea originated from a conversation among literature, design, and law students about Greek myths and the importance of their symbolic resonance. "Narciso" is a modern reinterpretation of the myth, which nonetheless aims to preserve and convey the visual magic of ancient tales and the universality of their meaning. The entire project was developed in approximately three months, with only two days dedicated to on-location shooting.
