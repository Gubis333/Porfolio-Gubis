Habit System

Speculative Design

What if your data could vote for you? Habit System is a new political voting concept where an artificial intelligence analyzes your digital footprint — such as data from social media — to automatically determine the party you “vote” for. Through the prototype, users can enter their Instagram username and see their “real vote,” a reflection of their daily actions, interactions, and interests.
