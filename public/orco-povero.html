L'Orco e il Povero

Illustrated Book

"L'Orco e il Povero" is a multimedia illustrative project inspired by Dolfo Zorzut's fairy tale, re-adapted for a younger audience. We developed various communication artifacts, including a series of illustrated plates and a narrative recipe book that reworks the story through imaginary dishes. To promote the project, we animated the illustrations, creating a teaser that conveys the evocative atmosphere of the story.
