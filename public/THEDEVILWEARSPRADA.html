MU Design Show

Berlin

August 2024

Media University

The MU Design Show is an annual event held at the end of the academic year at Media University of Berlin. This exhibition provides a platform for students to present their creative works to the public. It features a diverse range of projects, highlighting the innovative and artistic talents of the university's students. Attendees can experience firsthand the cutting-edge ideas and designs that students develop throughout the year.

During my Erasmus I was selected as art director to define the visual identity of the "Media University Design Show." I had the opportunity to coordinate a team of students in designing all visual assets and developing the event's complete design system.
